EnellyNFT Roadmap - March & April 2025

Phase 1: Community Building & Partnerships

March 2025

  • • Build and engage the EnellyNFT community.
  • • Launch our VIP Pass for early supporters for free using NFT2Me as the launchpad.
  • • Collaborate with OG projects on Hyperliquid like Hypers and Liquina for mutual promotion.
  • • Launch the EnellyNFT website with key details on utility and upcoming events.

Phase 2: Mint & Launch

Mid to Late March 2025

  • • Launch our EnellyNFT mint for free for VIP Pass holders as an airdrop and with a significant discount for FCFS whitelisted individuals and the public.
  • • Open staking for VIP Pass holders and EnellyNFT holders post-mint.
  • • Announce and distribute the first airdrop of Enelly tokens to holders.

Phase 3: Utility Expansion & Engagement

Early to Mid April 2025

  • • Launch the raffle and quest platform for EnellyNFT holders.
  • • Activate the 10 XP per day reward system.
  • • Announce and distribute the second airdrop of Enelly tokens to holders.
  • • Roll out the next batch of airdrops and expand engagement initiatives.

Phase 4: Full Platform Launch & Scaling

Late April 2025

  • • Officially launch the Enelly platform, accessible first to VIP Pass holders.
  • • Announce and distribute the last airdrop of Enelly tokens to holders.
  • • Continue collaboration efforts and engage with the community for scaling.
  • • Host special events, collaborations, and additional rewards for holders.
